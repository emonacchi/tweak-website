= Privacy Policy
== Your Privacy Rights
This Privacy Policy describes your privacy rights regarding our collection and use of your activity data. It applies to the tweak browser extension and website https://tweak-extension.com.
== How we collect information about you
We use Google Analytics to collect information about how you use the tweak extension, this includes only interactions with the extension. Google Analytics also collects information such as device type (mobile/desktop) and geographical location.
== How we use the personal information we collect
Our primary purpose in collecting analytics data is to track bugs and enhance the usability of the extension based on the usage patterns identified.
== How you can contact us about privacy questions
If you have questions or concerns regarding this policy, you should contact us by sending an email to help.tweak@gmail.com.
